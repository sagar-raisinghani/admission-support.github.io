What is ISTE-VESIT?

The Indian Society of Technical Education was founded in 1967 and the VESIT chapter of ISTE was instituted in the year 2008 and since then it has been a pioneer in the task of introducing novel events which are a balanced blend of technical and non-technical concepts. It provides a fantastic opportunity to all of its members to showcase their inherent talents and also serves as a training ground to cultivate overall personality and professional skills.

What does ISTE-VESIT do?

ISTE-VESIT conducts technical and non-technical events throughout the academic year and at the end of the year the winner of those events are felicitated at our Symposium. The major objective of the ISTE is to assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industries and other organisations. It provides guidance and training to students to develop better learning skills and personality.

Why to be a part of ISTE-VESIT?

Being a member of ISTE not only helps you to to develop your technical skills but also helps in overall holistic development of an individual. Once you become the member, You can be a part of the ISTE-VESIT council by going through the process of interviews.

How to become a member of ISTE-VESIT?

The membership amount of ISTE-VESIT is just Rs. 600 for 3 years. You can be a member by filling up the membership form and paying the membership amount. For membership, contact Rahul Bhatia (Public Relations Officer): 7666266974
